3rd Academic Semester Maps

Series of maps I have done for University Projects during my 4th Academic Semester.
Disclaimer: Language used is Romanian.

EN: Remote sensing and Fotointerpretation
RO: Teledetecție și Fotointerpretare

For this seminar I made a 40 page quantitative satelitary research of three different subjects.

Firstly, using Sentinel-2 imagery, I measured the water surface for three different timelines before and after the Lower Se San 2 Dam oficially opened in October 2017. Since the maps are in Romanian you probably wont understand much.

Secondly, using the NDWI and different false-color band combinations I managed to measure the surfaces of Vlădeni reservoirs.

Thirdly, I measured the surface growth of the movement areas (runways etc.) of the Iași International Airport after it's modernization. I georeferenced Google Earth historical imagery to do that. Also, using the NDVI generated from pansharpened Landsat imagery, I was able to find the approximate timeline the runway modernization began.

EN: Biogeography
RO: Biogeografie

For this seminar I had to make a presentation about the hot deserts of the Northern Hemisphere. This map shows the annual precipitation values of the North American deserts. I used worldclim.org data.
